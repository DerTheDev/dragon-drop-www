Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any HTML/CSS/JavaScript knowledge to make a website through DragonDrop?

No, you don't need any coding language to use DragonDrop. If you do know some you can put it in to your website though.

Will the website I create go online the second I create it?

Yes, the website will go online the instance you click that publish button!"

Can I connect my own domain to DragonDrop

Yes, Domain Transfer is a feature included in all Premium Purchases!"

Will I have DragonDrop Ads on my website?

Yes, but if you have any Premium Service then you can remove the ads!"!

What is the differences between Free, Standard, Pro, and Premium Services?

Standard and Pro Services are Premium Services. Premium Services are plans that are purchased, not
free. Premium Services provide emails for your website, a free domain for one year, no ads, domain
transfers, and many other features that the free plan does not include. The Pro plan contains all of
what the Standard Plan covers, but more! The Standard Plan costs $5.86, Pro costs $10.99!

Do I get a free domain with the FREE service?

You will get a site in the form of: nameofyoursite.dragondrops.net! To have your own site like
this: nameofyoursite.extension, then you must purchase Premium Services.

Do I get a free email with the FREE service?

No you will not. You must purchase Premium Services for email!
